Past Life Regression Theory (PLRT)

Explore Subconscious Memories & Heal Through the Wisdom of Past Lives

What is Past Life Regression Theory (PLRT)?

Past Life Regression Theory is a method used to explore memories believed to be connected with previous lifetimes through deep relaxation and guided visualization techniques. It helps individuals understand emotional patterns, fears, or life situations that may have deeper subconscious roots.

Why Should I Learn Past Life Regression?

Learning PLRT can help you understand how subconscious memories influence present life decisions, relationships, fears, and habits. It also allows you to guide others through safe regression techniques for emotional insight and self-awareness.

Do I Need Any Background to Learn This?

No. This course is designed for beginners as well as people who already study astrology, spirituality, psychology, or healing practices.

What Make This Course Different?

This course is designed with a structured learning approach, simplified explanations, and practical insights, making it easy for beginners to understand Past Life Regression without confusion.

What Will You Learn in this Course?

A structured PLRT course typically covers topics such as:

  • Fundamentals of Past Life Regression Theory
  • How subconscious memories influence present life
  • Basic regression techniques
  • How guided regression sessions work
  • Ethical guidelines and precautions
  • Real-life applications of PLRT

Can I Build a Career Using PLRT Knowledge?

Many practitioners integrate PLRT with astrology, healing practices, counseling, or spiritual guidance. While this course introduces the theory and methods, deeper practice and experience are needed to become a professional practitioner.

Who Should Take This Course?

  • Astrology students
  • Spiritual seekers
  • Healers and therapists
  • Tarot or energy practitioners
  • Anyone curious about past life concepts and subconscious patterns
