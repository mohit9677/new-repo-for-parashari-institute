What is Past Life Regression Theory (PLRT)?

Past Life Regression Theory is a method used to explore memories believed to be connected with previous lifetimes through deep relaxation and guided visualization techniques. It helps individuals understand emotional patterns, fears, or life situations that may have deeper subconscious roots.

Why Should I Learn Past Life Regression?

Learning PLRT can help you understand how subconscious memories influence present life decisions, relationships, fears, and habits. It also allows you to guide others through safe regression techniques for emotional insight and self-awareness.

Do I Need Any Background to Learn This?

No. This crash course is designed for beginners as well as people who already study astrology, spirituality, psychology, or healing practices.

Is Past Life Regression hypnosis?

Past Life Regression often uses deep relaxation or hypnotic techniques, but the focus is on accessing subconscious memories rather than controlling the mind. The person always remains aware and in control during the process.

Is Past Life Regression scientifically proven?

Past Life Regression is a subject studied in psychology, spirituality, and consciousness research. While many people report powerful experiences, it is generally considered an exploratory spiritual practice rather than a scientifically proven method.
